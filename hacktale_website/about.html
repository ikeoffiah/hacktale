Our Story

At Hacktales, we want to solve one of the biggest problems in cybersecurity: The talent gap.

Employers need to hire specialized cybersecurity workforce to ensure business continuity. Aspiring cybersecurity professionals need to understand the knowledge areas and skills required for these specialized roles, upskill, and get connected to the available jobs.

Hacktales provides tailored career mentorship and technical expertise to help individuals across Africa transition smoothly into cybersecurity and earn sustainable income by ensuring business continuity for fast-paced organizations.

Vision

A world where everyone can get ahead through cybersecurity.

Mission

Our mission is to develop the next generation of cybersecurity professionals across Africa. With our flexible mentorship model, life-long learners in different career paths can take on in-demand cybersecurity roles.
