Introducing Hacktales Academy

Hacktales Academy offers a 9-month internship program for graduates of the cybersecurity foundation program. In this program, interns will gain hands-on technical expertise and industry experience in a specific career path

To enable you to transition smoothly, we've developed industry-based curriculums for five specialized entry-level cybersecurity paths.
