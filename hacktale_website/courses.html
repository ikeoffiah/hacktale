A life-time opportunity to advance your career through cybersecurity.

Hacktales Academy offers a 9-month internship opportunity for graduates
of the cybersecurity foundation program. Over the course of the internship, you will develop
hands-on technical expertise and gain industry experience within a specific cybersecurity career path.

GET CAREER GUIDE

To facilitate a seamless transition, you'll work closely with seasoned engineers in any of these five in-demand cybersecurity paths.

Information Security Analyst

Regardless of your industry, be it Finance, Law, Healthcare, Real Estate, Energy, Power or Education sector, as a cybersecurity analyst you will identify potential threats within organizations and strategize on how to mitigate the associated risks.

Security Awareness Engineer

Security Awareness Engineers are primarily focused on user security. In this role, you will be responsible for improving the security awareness level of employees by adopting innovative training concepts that spark genuine interest and influence behavior change.

Penetration Testing Engineer

As a penetration testing engineer, you will be responsible for assessing the digital footprint of organizations. Penetration testing engineers purposefully attempt to exploit computer systems in order to detect and fix existing security loopholes ahead of cybercriminals.

Application Security Engineer

This role will require you to work closely with developers, product managers, and third-party teams to ensure that software applications are secure by design and meet industry and regulatory compliance standards.

Security Support Engineer

As a support engineer, you will use industry-standard tools to continuously monitor the information security systems within organizations to identify network issues and resolve them in real-time.
